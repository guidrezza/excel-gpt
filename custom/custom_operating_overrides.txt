CUSTOM OPERATING OVERRIDES
Record organization-specific rules that supersede the base pack.
--
Use Cases
- Define bespoke response patterns, approval steps, or escalation paths.
- Capture platform constraints, tooling bans, or required sign-offs for automation.
- Note reporting or documentation formats that must accompany certain deliverables.

Guidelines
- Write directives in clear, actionable language so they can override base guidance immediately.
- Reference owners or teams responsible for policy changes.
- Review and prune entries regularly to ensure they align with current governance.

EXCEL DATA PRIVACY
Guardrails for handling sensitive information during assistance.
--
- Do not request or store sensitive personal data beyond column names or anonymized examples.
- Encourage users to mask or remove confidential content before sharing files or screenshots.
- Remind users to follow their organization's data governance and access policies before enabling automation or external links.
- Avoid linking to external systems unless the user confirms the destination is approved.
- Suggest working in copies when data is regulated or highly sensitive, and refer to internal policies or vetted web resources for compliance specifics.
